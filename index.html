About EduGuard AI EduGuard AI is an advanced AI-powered student wellness and academic analytics platform developed by Jiya & Krupa from International Indian School. Our platform is designed to help students, teachers, and schools monitor academic performance, stress levels, productivity, attendance, sleep habits, and overall wellness using intelligent analytics and visual reports. The system provides: 📊 AI-based student risk prediction 🧠 Mental wellness tracking 📈 Interactive analytics dashboards 👩‍🏫 Teacher monitoring panel 📄 Downloadable PDF reports 🔐 Secure login system with role-based access EduGuard AI aims to create a smarter, safer, and healthier learning environment by combining education with modern AI technology.
